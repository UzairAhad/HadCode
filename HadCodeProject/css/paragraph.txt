Introduction

HeadCode was founded on 18th of May 2020 is a welfare organization under leadership of Uzair Ahad Magsi. It's headquarters are situated in Karachi with 6 branches spread all over the country. There are currently 502 employees working in the organization. On 21st May 2020, HeadCode and Fixit came on a mutual agreement over the partnership for the success of achievement of their goals.
